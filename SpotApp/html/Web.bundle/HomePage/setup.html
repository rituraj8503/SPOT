Setup

5 simple steps to make your life easier!

Step 1

Log In to Spot, or create your free account if you don't have one.

Step 2

After you have built your application, submit your high-level query to our SpotBot, including any cloud provider preferences, if needed.

Step 3

SpotBot will outline all the optimal costs for your specific situation.

Step 4

Then, select "Deploy" to deploy your application to the cloud!
